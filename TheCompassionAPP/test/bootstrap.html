WE CARE.

In November of 2019, a novel strain of Corona Virus family, SARS-CoV-2 starting infecting humans, and gave birth to a new disease, COVID-19. On 11th of March 2020, the Director-General of World Health Organization declared COVID-19 to be characterized as a Pandemic. Since 2020, until this day we have gone through multiple waves in all the parts of the world, we developed vaccines and tried our best get it supplied in all parts of the world and we have seen two Variants of Interest (VOIs) and five Variants of Concerns (VOCs), Omicron Variant being the most recent and alarming one. When the health-care systems around the world faced this unprecedented event, we came up with the 'The Compassion App.' to utilize all of our resources in a more optimum way.



WE CONCILE.

In this project the aim is to create a comprehensive and customizable solution for non-profit groups that are trying to reach out to their benefactors and clients amid COVID-19 in form of a Mobile application. With this Organizations can help patients with the resources they need in order to cope up with Disease such as COVID-19. A benefactor can be a Medical professional, a Pharmacist, a philanthropist or any person who wishes to help amid this pandemic. A client can be a person who is currently tested positive for COVID-19 with or without co-morbidities, an essential service personnel or any person dealing with mental health issues due to COVID-19.



WE CULMINATE.

We aim to make the ends meet. From a Doctor to a Patient, From a Pharmacist to a person in need of medications, from a care-giver to a care-receiver, from a Philanthropist to a rather dependent person. From someone who can help in the time of need, to someone, who is in need.
